ABOUT & RESUME

"Working in Film & TV Sound since 2007"
Alex Faucheux is a professional Sound Mixer and member of IATSE Local 7, based in Denver, Colorado. With nearly two decades of experience across documentary, scripted, reality, and commercial production, he has brought his craft to projects for Netflix, AMC, FX, Prime Video, PBS, CNN, HGTV, Nike, and more.

A Louisiana native and Full Sail University graduate, Alex began his career in post-production before transitioning to production sound in 2011. Since then, he has built a reputation for precision, adaptability, and deep technical expertise across all production environments.
